SEDES visualizations

These visualizations are sample outputs of SEDES, a system for analyzing metrical position in Greek hexameter.

Instructions: Select a text below. Once there, you can:

  1. Align the beginning of words to the sedes grid.
  2. Highlight the text according to the statistical frequency (z) of lemmata in sedes. The darker the shade, the less frequent the lemma in its metrical position.
  3. Hover over a word to see its data, e.g. its lemma, sedes, frequency of its lemma in its sedes (x), total occurrence of lemma (Σx), number of standard deviations from the lemma's mean average in its range of sedes (z), and its metrical shape.
  4. Click a word to produce the above data as well as a table of its lemma's frequency (x), percentile (x/Σx), and standard deviations (z) in the sedes grid.
