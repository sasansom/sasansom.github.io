SEDES visualizations

These visualizations are sample outputs of SEDES, a system for analyzing metrical position in Greek hexameter.

Click on a text below. Once there, you may do the following.

  1. Align the beginning of words to the sedes grid.
  2. Highlight the text according to the statistical frequency (z) of a word's lemma in its sedes. The darker the shade, deeper the color, or larger the bubble, the more unexpected the word's lemma in its position.
  3. Hover over a word in the line to see its lemma, sedes, frequency in its sedes (x), number of standard deviations from the lemma's mean average in its range of sedes (z), and its metrical shape.
